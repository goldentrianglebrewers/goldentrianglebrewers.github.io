# Makeshift Mashout

### Fermentables
- 5.5 lb White Wheat 
- 3 lb Munich Malt
- 2 lb Vienna Malt
- 8 oz Special B
- 4 oz Chocolate Wheat

### Hops
- .25 oz Sabro at 60 minutes

### Yeast
- White Labs WLP380 Hefeweizen IV Ale
