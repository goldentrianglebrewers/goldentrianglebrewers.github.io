# Sup Bruh

- OG 1.060
- FG 1.016
- ABV 5.8%

### Fermentables
- 8.5 lb Maris Otter
- 3 lb White Wheat
- 1 lb Munich
- .75 lb Caramel 120L
- .5 lb Chocolate Wheat
- .5 lb Roasted Barley

### Hops
- .5 oz Sabro at 30 minutes
- 1 oz Sabro at 10 minutes
- 1 oz Sabro at flameout

### Yeast
- Omega OYL-021 Hefeweizen Ale
