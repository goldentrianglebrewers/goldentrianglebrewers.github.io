# Dunkelweizen

- OG 1.056

### Fermentables
- 2 lb Pilsner 
- 1 lb Chocolate Wheat
- 1 lb White Wheat
- 5 oz Carafoam
- 4 oz Carafa I
- 4 oz Chocolate Malt


### Hops
- .1 oz Tradition at 30 minutes
- .2 oz Sabro at 15 minutes
- .4 oz Tradition at flameout

### Yeast
- White Labs WLP300 Hefeweizen Ale
