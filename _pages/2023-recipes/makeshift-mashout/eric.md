# Baltic Porter

- OG 1.082
- FG 1.012
- ABV 5.8%

### Fermentables
- 3 lb Munich II
- 3 lb Vienna
- 2 lb Pilsner
- 1 lb Crystal 60L
- 4.6 oz Roasted Barley

### Hops
- .48 oz Magnum at 30 minutes
- .48 oz Saaz at 30 minutes

### Yeast
- Fermentis 34/70
