# Mardi Bock

### Fermentables
- 8.5 lbs Pilsner Malt
- 4 lbs Vienna Malt
- 1 lb Caramel Malt 10L
- 0.65 lb Acidulated Malt

### Hops
- 0.70 oz Magnum @ 60 min
- 0.30 oz Saaz @ 15 min
- 0.70 oz Saaz @ 5 min

### Yeast
- Fermentis 34/70
