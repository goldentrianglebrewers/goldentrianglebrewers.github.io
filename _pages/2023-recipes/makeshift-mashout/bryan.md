# Makeshift Mashout Vienna

- OG 1.060
- FG 1.010
- ABV 6.6%

### Fermentables
- 5 lb Vienna
- 3 lb 8 oz Red X 
- 3 lb Pilsner
- 1 lb 8 oz Goldpils Vienna
- 8 oz Carapils

### Hops
- 1.5 oz Tettnanger at 60 minutes
- 1.5 oz Saaz at 15 minutes

### Yeast
- Fermentis 34/70
