# Vienna Lager

- OG 1.056
- FG 1.012
- ABV 5.8%

### Fermentables
- 5 lb Pilsner
- 6 lb Vienna
- 1.5 lb Munich II
- .5 lb Caramber
- .5 lb Victory
- .15 lb Blackprinz

### Hops
- .63 oz Magnum at 60 minutes
- 2 oz Saaz at 0 minutes

### Yeast
- Fermentis 34/70
